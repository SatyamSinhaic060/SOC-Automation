•	The webhook gets the information when the alerts are triggered in splunk .

•	The information received by webhook is sent to perplexity node that makes a report and send it to slack and gmail.

•	Simultaneously the data is sent to Jira for assigning the task to different level of SOC analyst based on the severity of the alert.

•	The ssh node are triggered based on the type of alert triggered in splunk to control the threat and respond to it as soon as possible.

•	The ssh node response is not the final response ,it is a quick response to control the threat so that the analyst can take proper action in due time.
